  ## What we cover

  Each episode picks 5 stories from ArXiv preprints and industry news — filtered,
  ranked, and discussed between two hosts. No summaries. Just what the development
  means and why it matters right now.

  Topics: robot learning, foundation models, manipulation, autonomous systems,
  AI policy, and the occasional hardware surprise.

  **Format:** ~10 minutes · Two hosts · Daily
